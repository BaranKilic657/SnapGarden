Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the SnapGarden FAQ page! Here, you'll find answers to common questions about getting started, managing your account, and making the most of our app. Whether you're new to SnapGarden or looking for specific guidance, we've got you covered. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out to our support team!

Getting Started

How do I start using SnapGarden?

Simply download the SnapGarden app from your preferred app store, create an account, and start snapping photos of your plants. The app will guide you through plant identification, care tips, and more.

What devices is SnapGarden compatible with?

SnapGarden is available for both iOS and Android devices. Make sure your device meets the minimum system requirements for the best experience.

Do I need any special equipment to use SnapGarden?

No special equipment is needed! All you need is your smartphone or tablet with a working camera to snap photos of your plants.

Can SnapGarden identify all types of plants?

SnapGarden can identify a wide variety of plants, including houseplants, garden plants, and even some wild species. However, we’re constantly updating our database to improve accuracy and expand coverage.

Account

How do I create an account?

You can create an account directly in the SnapGarden app by providing your email address and setting a password. Alternatively, you can sign up using your Google or Apple account for quick access.

Is my account information secure?

Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously. SnapGarden uses encryption to protect your data and does not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Can I use SnapGarden without creating an account?

Some features, like plant identification, may be available without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your plant data, set reminders, and access personalized care tips.

How do I reset my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Go to the account settings in the app, select "Delete Account," and follow the prompts. Please note that this action is permanent and will erase all your saved data.
